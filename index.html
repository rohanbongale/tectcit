Sigthseens in Dandeli

SupaDam

Supa Dam is built across Kali river in state of Karnataka in India and is the highest dam in Karnataka.Supa Dam is all year round destination to be enjoyed with your friends, family, and relatives.

Syntheri Rocks

Syntheri rock is one of the most attractive place Located in the centre of Dandeli Wildlife Sanctuary There is a small waterfall that gushes into a deep gorge which gives spectacular view from the top.

Backwater

Backwaters of Supa Dam. It's a perfect place to enjoy a sunset view point...

MoulangiEcoPark

Moulangi Falls is formed by Moulangi River and is famous as a picnic spot. It is one of the few locations near the city that promote adventure

Dinseypark

Dinseypark Eco-park, you can see popular Disney, comic, and cartoon characters statues with their descriptions.

Crocodile Park

These crocodiles can be found in the Kali River of the Wildlife Sanctuary. where you can notice many crocodiles in the Kali River.
